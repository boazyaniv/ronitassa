## Page Types

### 1. Home Page
**Purpose**: First impression, brand introduction, quick navigation to key sections

**Sections**:
- Hero with brand tagline and compelling imagery
- Product categories grid
- About snippet with CTA
- Trust signals (kosher certification, quality badges)
- Testimonials or social proof (if available)
- Contact CTA with WhatsApp/Phone
- Location and hours
- Instagram feed or gallery preview (optional)

---

### 2. About Page
**Purpose**: Tell the brand story, build trust

**Sections**:
- Hero with brand story
- Founder/owner story
- Quality philosophy
- Sourcing and suppliers
- Team introduction (optional)
- Behind-the-scenes images
- Values and principles

---

### 3. Product Category Pages
**Purpose**: Showcase products within each category

**Sections**:
- Category hero with description
- Grid of products/cuts
- Each product card includes:
  - Image
  - Name
  - Cut type/description
  - Suggested use
  - Quality indicator
- Category-specific tips
- Related categories

---

### 4. Contact Page
**Purpose**: Make it easy to get in touch and visit

**Sections**:
- Location with embedded map
- Opening hours
- Contact methods:
  - Phone with click-to-call
  - WhatsApp with direct link
  - Email
  - Contact form (optional)
- Social media links
- Directions/parking info

---

### 5. Recipes/Tips (Optional)
**Purpose**: SEO, engagement, value-add content

**Sections**:
- Recipe listing grid
- Individual recipe pages with:
  - Ingredients
  - Instructions
  - Recommended cuts
  - Images
  - Tips

---

### 6. Certifications & Quality
**Purpose**: Build trust through transparency

**Sections**:
- Kosher certification display
- Quality standards
- Sourcing transparency
- Temperature control
- Any awards or recognition

---

## Navigation Structure

### Primary Navigation (Desktop & Mobile)
- Home
- About
- Products (with dropdown/submenu)
  - Beef
  - Lamb
  - Chicken
  - [Other categories]
- Recipes (optional)
- Contact

### Secondary Navigation (Footer)
- Privacy Policy (if needed)
- Accessibility Statement
- Certifications
- Social Media Links

### Utility Navigation
- Phone (sticky on mobile)
- WhatsApp (sticky on mobile)
- Language selector (if multi-language later)

---

## Content Blocks / Components

### Reusable Components
1. **Hero Section**: Image background, heading, subheading, CTA
2. **Product Card**: Image, title, description, badge
3. **Category Grid**: 2-3 columns responsive
4. **Trust Badge**: Icon, label, description
5. **Testimonial Card**: Quote, name, rating
6. **Contact CTA**: Icon, method, link/button
7. **Image Gallery**: Grid with lightbox
8. **Info Box**: Icon, heading, text
9. **Map Embed**: Google Maps integration
10. **Form**: Contact form with validation

---

## User Flows

### Flow 1: Discover Product Category
Home → Click Category → View Products → Contact via WhatsApp

### Flow 2: Learn About Brand
Home → About → Quality/Sourcing → Contact

### Flow 3: Find Location
Home → Contact Info → View Map → Directions

### Flow 4: Get Recipe Ideas
Home → Recipes → Individual Recipe → Related Products → Contact

---

## Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 768px
- Tablet: 768px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px
- Wide: > 1440px

---

## RTL Layout Considerations
- All text flows right-to-left
- Navigation menus mirror (start from right)
- Product grids flow RTL
- Icons and arrows flip horizontally
- Form fields align right
- Reading order: right to left, top to bottom
