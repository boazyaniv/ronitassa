# Project Overview: רוני טסה - Boutique Butcher Shop Website

## Project Name
רוני טסה - Marketing Website for Boutique Butcher Shop

## Purpose
Build a modern, Hebrew RTL marketing website for "רוני טסה", a boutique butcher shop. The site will showcase the brand, products, quality standards, and contact information. **Phase 1 is marketing only** – no e-commerce or checkout functionality.

## Goals
1. Present a premium brand identity and story
2. Showcase product categories and cuts with rich descriptions
3. Build trust through transparency about sourcing, quality, and kosher certification
4. Drive customer engagement via phone, WhatsApp, and in-person visits
5. Provide excellent user experience on mobile and desktop
6. Achieve strong SEO for local Israeli searches
7. Meet accessibility standards for Hebrew RTL layout

## Scope

### In Scope - Phase 1
- Static marketing site with Hebrew RTL layout
- Home page with hero, brand story, trust signals
- Product showcase pages by category (beef, lamb, chicken, etc.)
- About page with story, sourcing, quality standards
- Contact page with location, hours, map, phone, WhatsApp
- Optional: Recipes/Tips section, Gallery, Press mentions
- SEO optimization, structured data, performance optimization
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Accessibility compliance

### Out of Scope - Phase 1
- E-commerce functionality (cart, checkout, payment)
- User accounts or authentication
- Online ordering system
- Inventory management
- Multi-language support (Hebrew only)
- Admin CMS (content will be managed via code/files initially)

## Success Criteria
1. Site loads in < 2 seconds on mobile
2. Lighthouse scores: Performance > 90, Accessibility > 95, SEO > 95
3. All user-facing text is professional Hebrew
4. RTL layout works perfectly across all browsers
5. Clear conversion paths to phone/WhatsApp contact
6. Local business structured data validates
7. Positive user feedback on brand presentation

## Constraints
- **Language**: Hebrew only, RTL layout throughout
- **Budget**: Minimize hosting and tooling costs
- **Timeline**: Phased delivery, design approval before implementation
- **Technology**: Modern, maintainable stack with excellent Hebrew support
- **Content**: User must provide brand assets, product info, photos

## Assumptions
- User will provide necessary content and brand materials
- Hosting and domain will be determined during planning
- Professional photography may be available or stock imagery will be used
- Kosher certification details will be provided if applicable

## Glossary
- **RTL**: Right-to-left layout for Hebrew text
- **Hero**: Large prominent section at top of home page
- **Structured Data**: JSON-LD markup for search engines
- **LocalBusiness**: Schema.org type for local business information
- **CTA**: Call-to-action button or link
