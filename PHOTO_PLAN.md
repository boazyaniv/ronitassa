# Photo Plan - רוני טסה Butcher Shop Website

## Overview
This document outlines the complete photo strategy for the butcher shop website, including image selection, licensing, technical specifications, and implementation guidance.

## Visual Style Guide

### Color Grading
- **Temperature**: Warm (+5-10 Kelvin) to create inviting, appetizing feel
- **Contrast**: Medium-high (1.15-1.25x) for premium look
- **Saturation**: Natural to slightly boosted (+5-10%) on reds/browns
- **Vignette**: Subtle (-10-15%) on hero images only
- **Clarity**: +10-15% for meat texture detail

### Composition Guidelines
- Clean, uncluttered backgrounds
- Natural wood, stainless steel, white marble preferred
- Warm, natural lighting - avoid harsh shadows
- Focus on texture and marbling
- Family-friendly, appetizing presentation

## Technical Specifications

### Export Sizes by Placement

**Hero Images** (Home, Category pages)
- Dimensions: 2400×1350px minimum
- Formats: AVIF (primary), WebP (fallback), JPG (legacy)
- Target size: 250-400 KB (AVIF), 300-450 KB (WebP), 350-500 KB (JPG)
- Responsive breakpoints: 2400w, 1920w, 1280w, 960w, 640w

**Section Images** (About, Features)
- Dimensions: 1600×1067px minimum
- Formats: WebP (primary), JPG (fallback)
- Target size: 150-250 KB (WebP), 200-300 KB (JPG)
- Responsive breakpoints: 1600w, 1200w, 800w, 600w

**Product Grid Thumbnails**
- Dimensions: 800×800px or 800×533px
- Formats: WebP (primary), JPG (fallback)
- Target size: 60-120 KB (WebP), 80-150 KB (JPG)
- Responsive breakpoints: 800w, 600w, 400w

**Mobile Thumbnails**
- Dimensions: 600×600px or 600×400px
- Format: WebP
- Target size: 40-80 KB
- Single size optimized for 1x and 2x displays

### Compression Settings
- **AVIF**: Quality 65-75, effort 4
- **WebP**: Quality 80-85
- **JPG**: Quality 82-88, progressive, optimized

## Cropping & Safe Areas

### Hero Images (16:9)
- **Safe zone for text**: Center 60% horizontal, center 40% vertical
- **Focal point**: Slightly below center (y: 0.45) for better composition
- **Mobile crop**: Maintains center subject, may lose edges
- **Desktop crop**: Full width, slight letterboxing acceptable

### Product Grid (4:3 or 1:1)
- **Safe zone**: Center 80%
- **Focal point**: Dead center (x: 0.5, y: 0.5) for symmetry
- **Padding**: 5-10% around subject
- **Consistency**: Same aspect ratio across category

### Section Images (3:2)
- **Safe zone**: Center 70%
- **Focal point**: Rule of thirds (x: 0.33 or 0.66, y: 0.4 or 0.6)
- **Text overlay**: Top or bottom third only

## Image Optimization Workflow

1. **Download** original from source at highest resolution
2. **Crop** to target aspect ratio, respect focal point
3. **Color grade**: Apply warmth (+8K), contrast (+15%), clarity (+12%)
4. **Resize** to largest breakpoint (2400px for hero)
5. **Export** AVIF at quality 70
6. **Export** WebP at quality 82
7. **Export** JPG at quality 85
8. **Generate smaller breakpoints** via batch script
9. **Verify** file sizes meet targets
10. **Update** manifest with actual sizes

## Accessibility & SEO

### Alt Text Guidelines
- Describe what's in the image, not just "butcher shop photo"
- Include relevant keywords naturally
- Keep under 125 characters
- Be specific: "Marbled ribeye steaks on wooden cutting board" not "meat"

### File Naming Convention
```
[page]-[section]-[variant]-[width].[format]
Examples:
- home-hero-primary-2400.avif
- beef-product-ribeye-800.webp
- about-heritage-hands-1600.jpg
```

### Structured Data
Include ImageObject schema on product pages:
```json
{
  "@type": "ImageObject",
  "contentUrl": "https://ronitassa.com/img/beef-ribeye-800.webp",
  "caption": "Premium aged ribeye steak",
  "creditText": "Photo by Kyle Mackie on Unsplash"
}
```
